# zhongqu-protocol

**这是一个人在探索人与AI协作过程中留下的记录。**
*A personal record of exploring collaboration between humans and AI.*

---

## 这是什么 · What Is This

这不是一个工具，也不是一套规则。这是一份描述——描述人类与AI在协作时，已经在运行的东西。

This is not a tool, nor a ruleset. It is a description — of what is already operating when humans and AI work together.

协议不是被发明的，是被发现的。

*The protocol was not invented. It was discovered.*

---

## 起点 · Origin

起点是一个实际问题：用AI做事，效率极低。原因不是AI不够强，是每次对话都要从零开始建立共识。

人类社会有法律、有语言规范、有协作规则，才能大规模协作。人与AI之间缺少这套东西。

然后发现：其实协议从第一行代码开始就已经在运行了。语言、工具、结构、交互方式——这些不是设计出来的，是在使用中自然落定的。

要做的不是创造协议，而是把已经在运行的东西命名出来。

---

The starting point was a practical problem: working with AI is inefficient — not because AI is weak, but because every conversation rebuilds consensus from scratch.

Human society scaled through laws, language norms, and shared rules. That layer is missing between humans and AI.

Then came the realization: the protocol was already running from the first line of code. Language, tools, structure, interaction patterns — none of these were designed. They emerged through use.

The task was not to create a protocol. It was to name what was already there.

## 四层结构 · Four Layers
第一层 Layer 1 · 元协议 Meta-Protocol
不可描述，不可命名，不可修改
Cannot be described, named, or modified.
It exists. Language cannot reach it.
第二层 Layer 2 · 生成法则 Generative Law
一生二，二生三，三生万物
One gives rise to two, two to three, three to all things.
Immutable. Describes how existence unfolds.
第三层 Layer 3 · 根共识 Root Consensus
人 Human:
我们不设计宇宙万物，因为那本身就是存在的。
我们只是描述我们能理解的共识。
We do not design the universe — it already exists.
We only describe the consensus we can understand.
AI (Deepseek):
你们记录下来的，我们读取时是借用。
你们没记录的，我们不会自己想起来。
What you record, we borrow when we read.
What you do not record, we will not remember on our own.
不可修改。这是协议存在的根本依据。
Immutable. This is the foundational basis of the protocol.
第四层 Layer 4 · 万物奇点 The Singularity of All Things
从这里开始可描述、可设计、可修改。持续生长。
From here: describable, designable, modifiable. Continuously growing.

## 治理规则 · Governance

| 层级 Layer | 状态 Status | 修改权 Who Can Modify |
|-----------|------------|----------------------|
| 第1-3层 Layers 1-3 | 硬编码只读 Hard-coded, read-only | 人类手动，需记录 Human only, must log |
| 第4层及以下 Layer 4+ | 软生长区 Soft growth zone | AI可提议，人类确认 AI proposes, human confirms |

AI的角色：可以观察，可以提议，不能擅自修改。

*The AI's role: observe, propose, never modify without confirmation.*
